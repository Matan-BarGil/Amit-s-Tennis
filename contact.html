Amit Ashual

Amit is the talented creator and artist behind the beloved manga "Amit's Tennis." At 18 years old, he hails from Ashqelon, Israel, and has always dreamed of becoming a famous mangaka. In 2021, Amit began working on another popular manga, "Henry Ofek," which is filled with action and adventure. However, Amit wanted to create a more personal and relatable manga, reflecting his life as a tennis player. Thus, "Amit's Tennis" was born, based on his real-life experiences. Amit is dedicated to continuing and improving "Amit's Tennis" and deeply appreciates everyone's support, hoping to keep sharing his journey for a long time to come.
